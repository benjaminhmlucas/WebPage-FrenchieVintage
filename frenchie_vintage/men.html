Men’s Clothing & Accessories

At French Vintage finding the not-so-common ensemble for the dapper gentleman is just as important to us as finding the rare gems of female fashion. Our inventory is curated from a wide variety of sources. Our stock includes rare items that go as far back as the early 1900's. Items such as shirts, pants, sweaters, vests, and jackets. Our Polices:

Shipping:

  • All orders will be shipped within 1-3 days of receipt with a tracking or customs number.
  • International: Please allow 3-6 weeks for delivery. Please note that you are responsible for all VAT, import fees and taxes imposed by your country.
  • PLEASE NOTE: Little French Dress is not responsible for lost packages, all domestic packages are shipped with tracking and international packages are mailed with a customs number.

Refunds and Exchanges:

SELECT CAREFULLY AS ALL SALES ARE CONSIDERED FINAL! Though all sales are considered final - returns are reviewed on a case by case basis and only accepted if there was a significant error in the listing. Returns due to poor fit are not accepted. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Additional Policies and FAQs:

  • Please measure for a proper fit before you buy. All measurements are taken lying flat, and doubled where necessary, please leave room for movement! If you need more measurements than I have provided, please ask before placing an order.
  • All items in VINTAGE CONDITION: Pre-owned goods may show minor signs of wear due to age. I always indicate any major flaws or blemishes, but tiny signs of wear (pilling, pinholes, fading) are normal with vintage items.
  • Sizing info: To ensure a good fit, it is recommended that you compare measurements of your own clothing to the ones in the listings. That will give you a good idea of what measurements are right for you. If you need any extra measurements, just ask!
