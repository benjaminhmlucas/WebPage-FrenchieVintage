About Frenchie Vintage

The nostalgia, unique detailing and quality of vintage garments is what inspires this shop's collection. We curate clothing and accessories from the turn of the century through the early 1990s. Quality, Style and Timeless Appeal are the rules we live by. Our ultimate hope is to make you feel empowered, connected and confident in what you wear by creating a sense of individuality within your wardrobe and home. I am so glad you took the time to check out my site! Don’t forget: Our inventory is always changing and each item is unique! If you see something you like then don’t hesitate to snatch it up! It may not be here next time you look! Are you planning an event and need to rent furniture with individual flare? Check out our sister site The French Eclectic!
